Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

1. Overview

Pun Dad Monium respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information in connection with our e-commerce brand and social media presence.

2. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information through this website. When you purchase our products through Etsy or TikTok Shop, those platforms handle your order information according to their own privacy policies.

3. TikTok Integration

We use TikTok's Content Posting API to publish product showcase videos to our own TikTok account. This integration:

  • Only accesses our own TikTok account
  • Does not collect, store, or process any TikTok user data
  • Is used solely for publishing our brand's promotional content
  • Does not track or identify TikTok users in any way

4. Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use cookies, analytics, or tracking technologies.

5. Third-Party Services

Our products are fulfilled through print-on-demand partners. When you place an order on Etsy or TikTok Shop, those platforms and our fulfillment partners process your information according to their respective privacy policies.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any information associated with our business operations. We do not store customer personal data on our own servers.

7. Children's Privacy

Our products and services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, reach out to us through our Etsy or TikTok Shop pages.
